Scheduling Your Horizons

TCU Computer Science, 2016-2017

Who are we?

Scheduling Your Horizons is a group of 5 students in TCU's senior design program. We're working together with professors and leaders at Texas Wesleyan University to help update their scheduling software for their Expanding Your Horizons program.

What are we doing?

Each year, Texas Wesleyan University puts on an event with the national organization Expanding Your Horizons to help get middle school aged women interested in STEM subjects. The students choose to go to several one hour classes in their favorite STEM fields and go to these classes over the course of a day. We're rewriting TWU's aging scheduling software that will create each girl's schedule for the day based on what her interests are. We're very excited for this project since it helps young women get involved and excited in some of the most challenging and rewarding fields out there!
